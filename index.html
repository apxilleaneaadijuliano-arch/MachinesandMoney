Introduction

Farming and fishing today depend on machines like tractors and boats. These tools make work faster, but they are very expensive. This site explains why the high cost of machines is a big challenge for small farmers and fisherfolk.

Root Cause Analysis

The main issue is unaffordability. Machines are costly because of manufacturing, import taxes, and lack of subsidies. Without support, farmers use older tools, which lowers productivity and income.
