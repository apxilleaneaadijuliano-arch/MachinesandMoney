Introduction

Agriculture and Fishery Arts (AFA) is a TLE learning area that teaches students practical skills using machines to improve efficiency and productivity in farming, fishing, and food processing. This website focuses on how the high cost of machines affects students’ hands-on learning.

Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

The main root cause of the problem is the high cost of machines. Prices are affected by advanced technology, import fees, and maintenance expenses. Schools often cannot afford to buy machines, and borrowing through partnerships is not always reliable. This limits students’ access to practical, hands-on learning.

Drones are modern agricultural machines that help improve efficiency but are often costly.
