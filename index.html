Introduction

This website explains the challenges of expensive machines in farming and fishing. We focus on how these costs affect small farmers and fisherfolk, making it harder for them to improve productivity.

Root Cause Analysis

The main problem is the high cost of machines. Farmers cannot afford modern equipment because of expensive manufacturing, import taxes, and lack of subsidies. This reduces efficiency and increases production costs.
